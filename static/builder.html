How to use this:

Basic Usage (BiS builds using all items)

  1. Select a unit to build BiS for
  2. Select a skill or a goal
  3. Click on the Build button
  4. ???
  5. Profit

Advanced Usage

More options
  • If you plan to use an attack with innate element(s) or your unit has been imbued with an element, you can select the corresponding element(s) in the "Skill innate element(s)" section (for instance, you'll want to select Ice if you plan to use Fryevia's Frost Flower Blitz.
  • Fill information on the monster you're facing, its race(s) and its elemental resistance (negative number for elemental weakness).
  • Take into account the elemental debufs you'll give it (for instance, if using Orlandeau's Divine Ruination, substract 50 from the light elemental resistance).
  • You can adjust the DEF/SPR of the monster to account for breaks.
  • Custom Formula allows to create tailored-made builds. More information on how to use it when you click on it.
  • Some pre-set custom formula can be found on the custom formula popup. Click on the arrow on the left of the text field to get access to various healing and expedition custom formula.
Equipment selection :
  • All Equipments :
    • All available Equipment, unless limited by available options.
    • Espers at Maximum rarity and level, with only Killers unlocked.
  • Only owned equipments :
    • Only available when logged in.
    • All equipments inputed in the inventory page.
    • If espers are inputed in the Espers page, will use that, else default to the same espers as All Equipments.
  • Only owned equipments available to expedition :
    • Only available when logged in.
    • Same as Only owned equipment, but exlude items marked as excluded from expedition in the inventory page.
    • No esper will be automatically selected.
    • For ease of use, pre-set custom formula can be found on the custom formula popup. Click on the arrow on the left of the text field to get access to the expedition custom formula
  • Only unlimited equipments (not event shop/recipe) :
    • Only items available at will from shop/recipe and not from event will be used.
    • No esper will be automatically selected.
Why log in ?
  • To make builds tailored just for your available equipments :
    • Log in and go to the Inventory page.
    • Take the time to input your inventory, and update it as necessary.
  • To allow this tool to suggest you TMR to farm :
    • Log in and go to the Units page.
    • Take the time to input your units, and adjust the available TMRs.
    • When building with "Only owned equipments", select "Include TMR of owned units not farmed yet"
  • To be able to use your own esper builds and not the default ones :
    • Log in and go to the Espers page.
    • Take the time to input your esper information.
    • Note: The Espers page uses "path finding." This allows you to select objects on the outer-most ring, and claim all objects along that path. For example, Selecting Ifrit's 75% Beast Killer selects a total of 9 objects, starting with Fire, for a total of 370 SP.
    • Note: To remove an added object, simply click on it again. Path finding works here as well, allowing entire sets of objects to be removed. For example, Ifrit's Beast killer can be trimmed back to the 50% killer by clicking on the ATK + 100 object that follows the 50% killer.

How to use this:

  • The "Maximize" field must contain an EXPRESSION, that the builder will try to maximize
  • The "With" field can contain a CONDITION that the builder will try to meet
  • An EXPRESSION must resolve to a number, so it can only contain + - * / and VALUE VARIABLES, no OR AND or CONDITIONS
  • A CONDITION must resolve to true or false. It can contain CONDITIONS assembled by AND OR, or be of the form EXPRESSION > CONSTANT
  • Some valid formula :
    Maximize HPMaximize HP
    Maximize DEF * HPMaximize the product of HP by DEF (physical eHP)
    Maximize SPR * HPMaximize the product of HP by SPR (magical eHP)
    Maximize MP * MP_REFRESHMaximize the number of MP regained each turn
    Maximize M_DAMAGE With HP > 6000Maximize Magical Damage while keeping HP > 6000
    Maximize P_DAMAGE With P_EVADE > 100 AND HP > 5000Maximize Physical Damage while reaching 100% physical evade and HP > 5000
    Maximize P_DAMAGE With I_PHYSICAL AND HP > 5000Maximize Physical Damage while reaching 100% physical evade and HP > 5000
    Maximize HP With LB > 12Maximize HP while insuring at least 12 LB points per turn
    Maximize P_DAMAGE With I_PHYSICAL AND i_PARALYSIS AND I_DEATH AND E_LIGHTFormula to carry Gilgamesh trial. 100% evasion, immunity to paralysis and death, Light elemental weapon and then maximize damage
    Maximize P_DAMAGE With E_LIGHT OR E_FIREMaximize physical damage with a light or fire weapon (or both)
  • The more different variables you use in your formula, the more time the calculations will take
  • Available variables :
    HP, MP, ATK, DEF, MAG, SPRValue of the corresponding basic stat
    ACCURACYAccuracy
    MP_REFRESH% of MP refresh per turn (values are in the 0.01 range)
    P_DAMAGEThis is the total physical damage, taking into account the ATK, the elements, the killers, the damage variance, ...
    M_DAMAGESame as P_DAMAGE but for magical damage
    H_DAMAGESame as P_DAMAGE but for hybrid damage
    J_DAMAGESame as P_DAMAGE but for jump damage
    S_DAMAGESame as P_DAMAGE but for summon damage
    F_DAMAGEPhysical type Fixed damage. Assume fixed damage of 1000
    P_DAMAGE_MAGSame as P_DAMAGE but use MAG scalling
    P_DAMAGE_SPRSame as P_DAMAGE but use SPR scalling
    M_DAMAGE_ATKSame as M_DAMAGE but use ATK scalling
    M_DAMAGE_SPRSame as M_DAMAGE but use SPR scalling
    F_DAMAGE_ATKFixed damage that use ATK scalling
    P_DAMAGE_MULTICASTPhysical damage used with W- or T-ability. Multiply it by 2 for W-cast, or by 3 for T-cast
    P_DAMAGE_MAG_MULTICAST, P_DAMAGE_SPR_MULTICAST, P_DAMAGE_DEF_MULTICASTSame as P_DAMAGE_MULTICAST, but respectively MAG, SPR or DEF scaling
    LB_XXXSame as XXX, but treated as a Limit Burst. For instance : LB_P_DAMAGE_MULTICAST for physical LB (they are not affected by DW)
    P_EVADEPhysical evade
    M_EVADEMagical evade
    EVO_MAGBoost to Evocation damage
    LBNumber of LB point generated each turn. Note that this number is heavilly dependant on the expected number of LB shards gained per turn.
    R_FIRE, R_ICE, R_LIGHTNING, R_WATER, R_EARTH, R_WIND, R_LIGHT, R_DARKCorresponding elemental resistance
    R_POISON, R_BLIND, R_SLEEP, R_SILENCE, R_PARALYSIS, R_CONFUSION, R_DISEASE, R_PETRIFICATION, R_DEATHCorresponding ailment resistance
    DRAW_ATTACKSdraw attacks
  • Condition variables and Condition shortcuts
    I_PHYSICALShortcut for P_EVADE > 100
    I_POISON, I_BLIND, I_SLEEP, I_SILENCE, I_PARALYSIS, I_CONFUSION, I_DISEASE, I_PETRIFICATION, I_DEATHI_XXX is a shortcut for R_XXX > 100
    I_AILMENTSThis condition is a shortcut for all R_<ailment> > 100
    I_DISABLEThis condition is a shortcut for I_SLEEP; I_PARALYSIS; I_CONFUSION; I_PETRIFICATION
    E_FIRE, E_ICE, E_LIGHTNING, E_WATER, E_EARTH, E_WIND, E_LIGHT, E_DARKThose special conditions will force the resulting build to have the corresponding element(s) and no other elements
    E_NONEForce a none elemental build. This condition is not compatible with E_FIRE, E_ICE, ...
