Custom Formula

How to use this:

  • The "Maximize" field must contain an EXPRESSION, that the builder will try to maximize
  • The "With" field can contain a CONDITION that the builder will try to meet
  • An EXPRESSION must resolve to a number, so it can only contain + - * / and VALUE VARIABLES, no OR AND or CONDITIONS
  • A CONDITION must resolve to true or false. It can contain CONDITIONS assembled by AND OR, or be of the form EXPRESSION > CONSTANT
